# Spontaneous Application

We are always looking for **talented and motivated people** to enrich our team, even if there is no open position that fits you right now.  

**What we are looking for:**  
- People passionate about technology, design, or other areas relevant to us  
- Creative minds who can work independently and enjoy contributing ideas  
- Team players who are eager to make an impact  

**How to apply:**  
Send your application via email to:  
**[jobs@ecrow.de](mailto:jobs@ecrow.de)**  

We will get back to you promptly and discuss how you could become part of our team.  

---

**Tip:** Please include in your email a short note about which area you are interested in and what motivates you, this helps us match you better.
